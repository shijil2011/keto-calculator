## Internal Linking Strategy
- Every page links to the **Keto Calculator** (homepage)
- Every page links to at least **2 related pages** within the same cluster
- Keto Basics pages link to each other
- Recipe pages cross-link to related food lists
- Medical pages link to the "Keto Macros Calculator"

## SEO Requirements Per Page
- **Word Count:** 2,000+ words
- **H1:** Primary keyword as exact match
- **First Sentence:** Primary keyword integrated
- **H2/H3:** Logical subheadings (LSI keywords)
- **Internal Links:** 3-5 contextual links
- **FAQ Schema:** Minimum 5 questions at bottom
- **Meta:** Title, description, keywords in metadata
